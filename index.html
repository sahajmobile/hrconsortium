About HR Consortium

HR Consortium is a USA-based company focused on creating opportunities in emerging markets. We believe that access to technology is a fundamental driver of economic growth and social development.

Our mission is to identify and support businesses that break down barriers to technology adoption, particularly in underserved communities. Through strategic investments and operational support, we help our portfolio companies achieve their vision of digital inclusion.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: Supporting groundbreaking approaches to market challenges
  • Accessibility: Making technology available to everyone
  • Empowerment: Enabling communities through digital tools
  • Sustainability: Building businesses with long-term social impact
